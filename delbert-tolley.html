Delbert "Papa" Newland Tolley

Delbert was born September 14, 1899, in Cranberry, Mitchell County NC.

His parents were Handy Sebastian and Mary Jane Woody Tolley.

In 1917-1918 his family moved to McDowell County WV where he met his wife. They were married on November 26, 1920. Together they had four children:

  • Delbert Newland (1921-1964)
  • Ruth and Cecil Crawley (1923-1987)
  • George and Hazel Tolley (1926-1997)
  • Christine and Victor Argabright (1929-2012)

There are no birth records for him. I have however found his military draft card.
